Paper Title
Analyzing the Dependency of ConvNets on Spatial Information

Review Question

1. [Summary]

​ This article modified the latter part of the CNN by using spatial shuffle and GAP+FC. Though it destroyed the spatial information, the performance can still be maintained. The author did experiment on several classification and detection task and got similar results. This article can let us take a fresh look at how CNN works.

2. [Strengths]

  • Easy to read and understand
  • Idea is refreshing and is contrary to common sense. This can help to study the fundamental problem of neural networks
  • The experimental results can reflect the viewpoint well. The result is widespread and obvious

3. [Weaknesses]

  • Experiments are not sufficient enough. To show the universality of this phenomenon, you should do more experiments on more datasets or models
  • In Table 2. the performance of parameter reduction is not good enough. There are no obvious advantages for using GAP+FC
  • Channel shuffle and GAP+FC are not original ideas. There is no obvious better place to work than someone else
  • In Figure 7. The result of patch-wise shuffle doesn’t make much sense and the picture is not beautiful enough

4. [Overall rating]

  • Borderline

5. [Justification of rating]

As I said above, the Idea is refreshing, however, the result is plain. Unless you can show more impressive result, it is hard to give a accept.

6. [Detailed comments]

What are the strengths of your work compared with other work such as ShuffleNet? Please explain what is the use of such findings?

7. [Confidence]

  • Somewhat confident

8. [Reproducibility]

  • no code submitted

9. [Private comments to AC]

  • None
